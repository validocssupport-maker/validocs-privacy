Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the ExpatWay - KSA Expat Companion app for Android mobile devices, operated by Salman Aslam (the "Service Provider"). This policy is effective as of 2026-06-08.

ExpatWay is a document expiry tracking and reminder app built specifically for expats living in Saudi Arabia. The app helps you track Iqama, Passport, SCE membership, SOCPA membership, SCFHS licence, Ejar rental contracts, Driving Licence, Istimara, Fahas, and other personal and family documents.

What Information We Collect

The Application collects the following information:

  • Your email address and display name — when you create an account or sign in with Google
  • Document information you voluntarily enter — document types, expiry dates, document numbers, and notes
  • Profile photos you voluntarily upload for family members
  • Document images you voluntarily attach to your document records
  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Your mobile operating system and device information
  • Time and date of app usage
How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide document expiry tracking and reminder functionality
  • Schedule and deliver push notification alerts before your documents expire
  • Enable cloud backup and restore of your document data across devices
  • Allow you to manage family and team member documents within the app
  • Improve the Application's performance and functionality
Local Storage

All document data you enter is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (device local storage). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally unless you explicitly choose to enable cloud backup. Local data includes your document records, member profiles, workspace settings, theme preferences, and notification settings.

Cloud Backup (Optional)

Cloud backup is entirely optional. It is only activated when you choose to sign in and use the backup feature. When cloud backup is enabled:

  • Your document details are securely stored in Google Firebase Firestore under your unique user account
  • Each user's data is isolated — only accessible through their authenticated account
  • Document images are stored on your device only and are never uploaded to the cloud
  • Cloud data can be restored to any device by signing in with the same account
Google Sign-In

The Application offers optional sign-in using Google Sign-In, a service provided by Google LLC. When you use Google Sign-In:

  • Google processes your authentication in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy
  • The Service Provider receives only your name and email address from Google to create and manage your account within ExpatWay
  • The Service Provider does not receive or store your Google password
  • You can revoke Google Sign-In access at any time from your Google Account settings
Push Notifications

The Application uses locally scheduled push notifications to alert you before your documents expire. These notifications are scheduled directly on your device — they do not require sending your personal data to external servers. Notification permission is requested on first launch and can be withdrawn at any time through your device Settings.

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services — required for Android app functionality
  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore Database) — used for account authentication and optional cloud backup
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication method
Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Application uses local device storage (AsyncStorage) to save your preferences and data. The Application does not use browser cookies, advertising trackers, or cross-app tracking technologies. Firebase SDKs may collect anonymised usage statistics in accordance with Google's data processing terms. The Application contains no advertisements.

Data Sharing

The Service Provider does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be disclosed only in the following circumstances:
